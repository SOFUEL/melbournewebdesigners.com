How Long Does a Website Take to Build for Melbourne Businesses?

By the MelbourneWebDesigners.com editorial desk · 24 July 2026 · 2 min read

Building a website for a Melbourne business typically takes 4 to 12 weeks in 2026, depending on the complexity and specific requirements. For a standard business website, expect a timeline closer to 4-8 weeks, while more intricate e-commerce sites might stretch to the upper range.

What are the Typical Timelines for Building a Website in Melbourne?

In Melbourne, creating a basic business website often spans 4 to 8 weeks. This timeframe allows for planning, design, development, and testing. E-commerce sites are more complex, requiring 8 to 12 weeks due to additional features like payment systems and inventory management.

What Factors Influence the Duration of a Website Build?

Several factors can influence how long it takes to build a website:

  • Scope and Complexity: More pages and features mean more time.
  • Content Availability: Delays in content provision can stall progress.
  • Feedback Cycles: Frequent revisions can extend timelines.
  • Resource Allocation: More developers can speed up the process, but agency rates range from A$150-200/hr.

How Do Different Platforms Affect Website Development Time?

The choice of platform significantly impacts development time:

  • Shopify: Quick setup for e-commerce, often 2-4 weeks. See Shopify pricing.
  • Webflow: Offers flexibility for design-focused sites, typically 4-8 weeks. Check Webflow pricing.

For a deeper dive into choosing the right platform, explore our guide on how to choose a web designer in Melbourne.

What are the Stages Involved in the Website Building Process?

Building a website involves several key stages:

  1. Planning: Define goals, audience, and scope.
  2. Design: Create wireframes and design drafts.
  3. Development: Code the website and integrate functionalities.
  4. Testing: Ensure everything works across devices and browsers.
  5. Launch: Deploy the site and perform final checks.

How Can Businesses Expedite Their Website Development?

To speed up the process, businesses can:

  • Prepare Content Early: Have text and images ready before development starts.
  • Limit Revisions: Make consolidated feedback to minimize back-and-forth.
  • Use Templates: Opt for pre-designed templates to save time.

What Common Delays Should Melbourne Businesses Anticipate?

Common delays include:

  • Content Delays: Not having content ready can stall the design phase.
  • Technical Issues: Unforeseen technical challenges during development.
  • Feedback Delays: Slow responses during review stages.

How Does Project Scope Impact the Timeline for Website Completion?

The scope of a project is a major determinant of its timeline. A simple informational site might need only a few weeks, while a large-scale e-commerce platform with custom features could take several months. Businesses should clearly define their project scope from the outset to ensure realistic timelines.
